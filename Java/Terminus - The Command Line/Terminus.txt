1. What are your options in the "SpellCastingAcademy"?

Locations: PracticeRoom, Lessons
Items: HurryingStudent

2. What does the "less" command do?

Interact with items in the world.

3. What can you do with a "PracticeDummy"?

You can practice new spells on a Practice Dummy, and you can also move it into a box.

4. What happens if someone tries to ‘mv’ a PracticeDummy that is no longer in the PracticeRoom?

It is no longer a valid item to move.

5. What do you see when you try to look into a box (ls Box)?

You can see the items you have placed in it (I put PracticeDummies in it).

6. What is in the "Green Meadow"?

There is a pony in the green meadow.

7. Who is in the cave in the EasternMountains?

There is an old man in the cave in the EasternMountains.

8. What do the OldManuscripts teach you to do?

The OldManuscripts teach you how to use the 'help' and 'man' commands.

9. What is surprising about the rat?

It is a very large rat, the size of a small dog.

10. Where are you brought to when you use the portal?

You are transported to TownSquare back in time when you use the portal.

11. What do you learn when researching the dark wizard?

The power of "sudo" may be his only weakness.

12. What does the vendor reveal when smiling?

He tells you about the "rm" and "mkdir" spell.

13. Name two things you can interact with at the farm?

EarOfCorn, Farmer

14. What is in the cage?

A scared-looking kid.
